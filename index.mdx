## Introduction

Seminode is designed to power a new generation of business-to-business material trade in the electronics industry by delivering an open, connected, and AI-ready infrastructure for global supply chains. Seminode streamlines the way your business buys or sells electronic components by connecting you directly with customers and vendors through a modern, efficient network eliminating the delays and confusion of spreadsheets and emails.

To get started with Seminode, sign up for an account or receive an invite from your organization. Once logged in, you'll have access to create relays, establish connections, and begin exchanging data with your business partners.

## Key Concepts

- **Relays:** Permissioned databases that allow you to post and access supply or demand data.  
- **Connections:** Established channels between your relays and customers or vendors in your network.  
- **Lists:** Standardized data formats for component supply and demand.  
- **Network Feed:** Real-time streams of data from your connections across your network.  
- **Quoting System:** A structured workflow for negotiating and closing orders.
